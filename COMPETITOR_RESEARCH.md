## 1. Gojiberry AI (Direct Competitor)

**What they do:** Find & contact high-intent leads using 10+ LinkedIn intent signals (funding, new roles, events, competitor engagement). AI agents filter by ICP and run smart outreach campaigns, booking demos automatically.

**Revenue (Stripe-verified on TrustMRR, updated March 2, 2026):**
- All-time revenue: **$275,245**
- Reported MRR: **~$155K** (note: may include active trials)
- Real paid MRR estimated around **$85K** (per industry commentary)
- **1,477 active subscriptions**
- Growth: 36% MoM MRR growth, 55% client count increase, churn dropped 30%
- Target: $1M ARR (announced by co-founder Romàn Czerny)

**Pricing:** Pro $99/mo per seat | Elite $249/mo per seat

**Team:** Co-founders Pierre-Eliott Lallemant & Romàn Czerny (previously built Coco AI, bootstrapped to €500K ARR, sold to US fund)

**GTM Strategy:** 5,000 cold emails/day + 5 LinkedIn accounts sending ~800 high-intent DMs/week. Claims 31% reply rates vs industry standard 1-2%.

## 2. Sally AI

**What they do:** AI SDR that researches prospects 24/7 and crafts personalized outreach copy. Tracks prospects in real-time, identifies signals (product launches, job changes, job postings) and generates personalized outreach.

**Revenue:**

- Founded 2015, $0 in outside funding (bootstrapped)

**Pricing:** Not publicly disclosed

## Competitive Landscape Summary

### Market Tiers

**Tier 1 — Platform Leaders ($50M+ ARR):**
- Clay (~$100M ARR, $5B valuation)
- Apollo.io (~$100M+ ARR)

**Tier 2 — Funded AI SDRs ($1M-$10M ARR):**
- Artisan AI (~$5M ARR, $46M raised)
- Sally AI (~$2.5M revenue, bootstrapped)
- AiSDR (~$500K revenue, targeting $2M ARR)
- Unify ($40M raised, revenue undisclosed)

**Tier 3 — Emerging/Bootstrapped (<$1M ARR):**
- Gojiberry AI (~$85K-$155K MRR, bootstrapped)
- SalesMind AI (early-stage, bootstrapped)
- Salee, Outreachly, Closely

**Tier 4 — LinkedIn Automation Tools (established):**
- Dux-Soup, Waalaxy, HeyReach, Expandi, Dripify, etc.

### Key Trends (2026)
1. **AI SDR convergence** — Tools moving from simple automation to full AI agents that research, personalize, and follow up autonomously
2. **Intent signals** — Gojiberry's approach of monitoring 10+ LinkedIn signals is becoming table stakes
3. **Quality > quantity** — Market shifting from mass outreach (1% reply) to targeted outreach (15-31% reply)
4. **Multi-channel** — LinkedIn + email + phone combining into unified sequences
5. **LinkedIn tightening limits** — Making safe, compliant automation critical
6. **Pricing pressure** — Range from $10/mo (basic automation) to $2,000/mo (full AI SDR)
